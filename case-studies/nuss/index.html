Overview

AI writing designed for Arabic first — not as an afterthought.

nuss is an Arabic-first writing workspace built for Arab writers, students, and researchers. Unlike most AI tools that treat Arabic as secondary, nuss was designed from the ground up with RTL workflows, Arabic NLP, and Quranic knowledge integration.

Our team designed and built the complete product — the writing interface, AI integrations, and the knowledge tools that make it uniquely valuable for Arabic speakers.

What it does

Write, transcribe, search — all in Arabic.

Transcribe audio and YouTube videos to Arabic text with high accuracy. Chat with your documents and ask questions in Arabic. Search any Quran verse and insert it directly into your writing with a simple command.

Free to start, no credit card required. Nuss is live at nuss.ink and serving Arabic writers worldwide.
